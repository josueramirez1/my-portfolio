Background and Projects


I received a Master of Science in Special Education in 2018 and have taught over 100 students with disabilities of varying degrees. With this background, I have developed a strong desire to use my creativity to build applications and experiences that improve people's everyday lives—especially for those who require further support and/or accessibility to access content.
